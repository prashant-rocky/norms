Steak Specials You Don’t Want to Miss

From juicy sirloins to sizzling T-bones, NORMS has been serving up unbeatable steak dinners at prices that keep our guests coming back for more.

A Tradition of Sizzling Flavor

Steak has been at the heart of NORMS since the beginning. We built our reputation on quality cuts grilled to perfection, served hot and hearty — and always at a value guests can trust. Our steak specials are a celebration of this tradition, delivering big flavor without breaking the bank.

What Makes Our Steaks Special?

  • Quality Cuts: From sirloin to ribeye, we choose cuts that deliver tenderness and rich flavor.
  • Expert Grilling: Cooked just the way you like — juicy, charred, and always satisfying.
  • Full Plates: Served with sides like crispy fries, fresh veggies, and our famous hotcakes or soup and salad.
  • Unbeatable Value: Big portions, small prices — that’s the NORMS way.
“Steak dinners that taste like a splurge — without the splurge.”

Fan Favorites

Guests rave about our New York Steak and Eggs, the sizzling Sirloin Special, and our hearty T-bone platters. Whether it’s breakfast, lunch, or dinner, our steak specials are designed to satisfy cravings around the clock.

Why Guests Keep Coming Back

It’s more than just steak — it’s the experience. Friendly service, a casual setting, and the knowledge that every bite will deliver. Families, friends, and late-night diners all know that when it comes to steak specials, NORMS never disappoints.

Ready for a sizzling deal? Stop by your local NORMS and sink your fork into one of our legendary steak specials today. Because steak this good shouldn’t be missed.

Since 1949, NORMS has been serving Southern California as the go-to neighborhood diner. Known for big portions, great value, and welcoming service, NORMS offers all-day breakfast, lunch, and dinner—24/7.
