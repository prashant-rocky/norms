Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

NORMS (https://norms-us.netlify.app/) may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and reservation details when you interact with our website, including contact forms and booking systems.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used to provide services, process reservations, respond to inquiries, improve our website, and communicate promotions or updates relevant to NORMS customers. We do not sell your information to third parties.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site usage, and tailor content. Please refer to our Cookies Policy for more details.

4. Data Security

NORMS implements reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no online platform can guarantee absolute security.

5. Sharing Information

Your information may be shared with trusted service providers who support our operations (e.g., reservation platforms, payment processors). We ensure that such partners comply with privacy standards.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. You may also opt out of marketing communications at any time by contacting us at ccparequests@norms.com.

7. Updates to Privacy Policy

NORMS may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page with the revised date. Continued use of the website indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
