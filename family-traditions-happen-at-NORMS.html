Family Traditions Happen at NORMS

Generations of families have gathered around our tables. Here’s why NORMS feels like home.

A Table for Everyone

Since 1949, NORMS has been more than just a diner — it’s been a meeting place for families of all shapes and sizes. From Sunday brunch with grandparents to late-night milkshakes with the kids, families have made NORMS part of their everyday lives. Our booths have held birthday celebrations, after-game team dinners, and countless small moments that turn into lasting memories.

Why Families Choose NORMS

  • Comfortable Atmosphere: A casual, welcoming space where kids, parents, and grandparents feel at ease.
  • Something for Everyone: From our famous Bigger Better Breakfast® to steaks, burgers, and salads — there’s a dish for every appetite.
  • Affordable Dining: Good food at a fair price means families can enjoy eating out together, any day of the week.
“NORMS isn’t just where we eat — it’s where we celebrate milestones, share laughs, and feel like family.”

Generations of Traditions

It’s not unusual to see three generations at a NORMS table. Parents who grew up eating pancakes at NORMS now bring their own children, continuing a cycle of community and tradition. Over time, these visits become a part of family identity — a shared experience passed down, just like recipes in a family cookbook.

Why NORMS Feels Like Home

Families don’t just return for the food — they return for the feeling. The warm service, familiar flavors, and welcoming vibe make every visit special. At NORMS, there’s always a seat at the table, and always room for one more guest. That sense of belonging is what makes us more than just a restaurant — it makes us family.

Whether it’s a casual weeknight dinner or a weekend celebration, NORMS has been part of Southern California families for decades. Pull up a chair — there’s always room for you at our table.

Since 1949, NORMS has been serving Southern California as the go-to neighborhood diner. Known for big portions, great value, and welcoming service, NORMS offers all-day breakfast, lunch, and dinner—24/7.
