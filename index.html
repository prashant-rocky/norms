About NORMS

Serving Since 1949

Founded in 1949, NORMS has been a Southern California icon for over 70 years. What started as one of Los Angeles’ first 24/7 diners has grown into a beloved tradition of great food, big portions, and unbeatable value.

From our famous hand-cut steaks to our all-day breakfast favorites, NORMS is where Life Happens. With over 20 locations across Southern California and our newest diner in Las Vegas, we continue to serve comfort food with a smile—day or night.
