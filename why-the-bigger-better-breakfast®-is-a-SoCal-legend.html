Why the Bigger Better Breakfast® is a SoCal Legend

Discover the story behind our all-time favorite breakfast that has fed millions since 1949. From its humble origin at our La Cienega diner to becoming a late-night staple across Southern California, this plate carries a taste of tradition in every bite.

The Origin of a Classic

When NORMS opened on La Cienega Boulevard in 1949, our goal was simple: serve great food at a great value — any hour of the day. The Bigger Better Breakfast® was created to deliver a complete, satisfying meal that could fuel a hardworking morning shift or cure a late-night craving. Its generous portions and predictable comfort quickly made it a go-to for families, night owls, and regulars who treated NORMS like a second home.

What’s On the Plate?

The Bigger Better Breakfast® is built to satisfy. Most plates include two eggs cooked your way, a choice of bacon, sausage, or ham, golden hash browns or home fries, and a stack of buttermilk hotcakes or toast. It’s a full meal, made fresh to order — and that combination of choice and quality is what keeps guests coming back.

“Not just breakfast — it’s a full meal that sets the tone for your day (or night).”

Why It Stands Out

  • Consistency: For over 70 years, guests know what they’ll get — hearty, fresh, and delicious.
  • Value: A complete, filling meal that offers more for less — the kind of comfort food that doesn’t strain the wallet.
  • Community: NORMS has always been a neighborhood gathering place. The Bigger Better Breakfast® is part of the rituals that bring people together.

Memories & Moments

People don’t just come to NORMS for food — they come for the moments. From families with three generations at one table to exhausted shift-workers finding solace in a plate of hotcakes at 2 a.m., the Bigger Better Breakfast® is woven into daily life across Southern California.

Keeping the Tradition Fresh

While the core of the breakfast has stayed the same, NORMS continually adapts: small menu refreshes, improved ingredients, and a renewed focus on experience ensure that every plate feels familiar yet carefully prepared. That balance of heritage and attention to detail keeps the Bigger Better Breakfast® timeless.

Want to try it yourself? Visit your local NORMS any time — we’re open 24/7 at most locations — and treat yourself to the Bigger Better Breakfast®. It’s more than a meal; it’s a SoCal tradition.

Since 1949, NORMS has been serving Southern California as the go-to neighborhood diner. Known for big portions, great value, and welcoming service, NORMS offers all-day breakfast, lunch, and dinner—24/7.
